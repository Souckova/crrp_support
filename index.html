  1. - Commands /me, /do
    1. /me
      • Purpose: It is used to describe what a character is doing or how they are behaving.
      • Display: Martin_Novak lighting a cigarette.
      • Example usage:
        • /me pulls an ID card out of his pocket and hands it to the police officer.
        • /me frowns and takes a step back.
    2. /do
      • Purpose: Used to describe external circumstances, emotions, states or outcomes that may not be directly apparent from the game mechanics.
      • Display: [DO] A knife would have been found in the pocket.
      • Example of use:
        • /do He would be holding a police officer's badge in his hand
        • /do There would be a broken phone on the table.
